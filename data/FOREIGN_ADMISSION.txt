PROGRAMS OFFERED TO FOREIGN STUDENTS
Foreign/international students can apply to the same programs as local students. BS (undergraduate): Aerospace Engineering, Avionics Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Materials Science and Engineering, Computer Science, Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Space Science, Physics, Mathematics, Biotechnology, Chemistry (Nanotechnology), Remote Sensing and GIS. MS and PhD: Aerospace, Electrical, Materials, Mechanical, Computer Science, Mathematics, Physics, Astronomy and related disciplines. Same program list as domestic admissions. Eligibility per program as on IST website.

FEE FOR FOREIGN STUDENTS
International/foreign students: Fee usually charged in USD. Approximately 5,000 US dollars or more per semester (subject to revision). Fee structure for foreign students is on IST website. All fees and charges subject to revision. Financial aid for international students: very limited; mostly for local students.

AL KHWARIZMI SCHOLARSHIPS FOR FOREIGN STUDENTS
Scholarships available for foreign students in BS, MS, and PhD programs at IST. Duration: BS up to 4 years, MS up to 2.5 years, PhD up to 4 years. Covers: university tuition fee as per actual; stipend PKR 15,000 (BS), 20,000 (MS), 35,000 (PhD) per month; one-time return air ticket. NOT covered: admission fee, endowment fund, library development fund, service charges, smart campus charges, hostel charges. Contact admissions for details.

ELIGIBILITY FOR FOREIGN APPLICANTS
Must meet eligibility requirements for respective program (BS, MS, PhD) as on IST website. Must be able to pay semester dues and allied charges as per fee structure for foreign students. Must be proficient in English (all lectures, exams, notifications at IST are in English). Must obtain equivalence certificate from IBCC or HEC Pakistan before closing date of application. Must obtain No Objection Certificate (NOC) from their Ministry of Foreign Affairs or Embassy in Pakistan.

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED
Personal statement in English (self-introduction, study/work experience, reasons for application, study plan or research proposal). Two letters of recommendation from full or associate professors in English (with signature, phone, email). Educational documents. Equivalence from IBCC/HEC. NOC from home country.

PROCEDURE
Apply online at www.ist.edu.pk. Complete application form and deposit application processing fee before last date. Merit determined per IST website criteria. Merit lists for BS and MS on website; PhD candidates intimated by email. Selected candidates deposit admission dues to secure seat. Cases referred to HEC and government for NOC/approval. Visa: approach Pakistani mission abroad with Student Visa Form, passport, photograph, educational documents, admission letter, NOC from home country.
