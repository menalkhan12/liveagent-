=== I. GENERAL ADMISSIONS & DEADLINES ===

When do admissions open at IST? Usually in March or April for the Fall semester.

Does IST offer Spring admissions? Typically, BS programs only open for Fall; MS/PhD may open in Spring.

Where can I apply? Exclusively through the IST Online Admission Portal.

Is there an age limit for undergraduate programs? Generally, there is no age limit for BS admissions in Pakistan.

How many programs can I apply for? You can select multiple programs (Aerospace, Avionics, CS, etc.) in a single application.

Can I apply via post? No, most top-tier Pakistani universities including IST only accept online applications.

When is the last date to apply? Usually late June or early July.

Do I need to visit the campus to apply? No, the entire process is digital.

How do I track my application? Use your Application Reference Number (ARN) on the portal.

What if I miss the deadline? Late applications are rarely entertained due to high competition.

=== II. ELIGIBILITY & ACADEMIC REQUIREMENTS ===

What is the minimum percentage for Engineering? Minimum 60% in FSc (Pre-Engineering) or equivalent.

Can Pre-Medical students apply for Engineering? Yes, if they take the Additional Mathematics paper.

Can ICS students apply for Engineering? Yes, for most programs including Aerospace and Avionics at IST.

What is the eligibility for Computer Science? Minimum 50–60% in HSSC with Mathematics as a subject.

Are A-Level students eligible? Yes, provided they have an IBCC Equivalence Certificate.

How is the merit calculated at IST? Usually a weightage of SSC (10%) + HSSC/Part-1 (40%) + Entry Test (50%).

What if my FSc Part-II result is not out? You can apply based on FSc Part-I results (Provisional Admission).

Is 50% in FSc enough? For Engineering, no. You must have at least 60% per PEC rules.

Does IST accept DAE students? Yes, in relevant technologies for reserved seats (check the prospectus for specific quotas).

Can I apply if I have a Supply in FSc? No, you must have a clear result at the time of final merit list.

=== III. ENTRY TESTS ===

Does IST conduct its own entry test? No, IST accepts results from tests like NAT, HAT, NET, ECAT, or ETEA.

Which test is best for IST? NAT-1E (conducted by NTS) is the most common, but NET (NUST) is also widely used.

Is the NTS-NAT mandatory? No, you can use HAT (HEC) or provincial tests like ECAT (UET) or ETEA.

Can I use my SAT scores? Yes, IST has seats for ACT/SAT international and national categories.

Is there negative marking in the NAT? No, unlike the ECAT or some other tests.

What is the validity of the Entry Test? Usually one year.

What is the minimum score required in NAT? You must score at least 33% to even be considered for the merit list.

Can I improve my NAT score? Yes, you can take NTS-NAT multiple times and submit the best score before the IST deadline.

Where do I send my test scores? You must enter the score in your IST online portal and upload the result card.

Does IST accept the FAST (NU) Entry Test? Generally, they stick to the tests listed on their official website (NTS, HEC, NUST, UET).

=== IV. IST SPECIFIC PROGRAMS ===

What is the difference between Aerospace and Avionics? Aerospace focuses on aircraft structures and engines; Avionics focuses on electronics and flight control systems.

Is IST accredited? Yes, all engineering programs are accredited by the Pakistan Engineering Council (PEC) under the Washington Accord (Level II).

Is Space Science different from Aerospace? Yes, Space Science is a BS (4-year) program focused on astronomy, remote sensing, and GIS.

Does IST offer Computer Science? Yes. The Computer Science (Computing) department offers BS Computer Science, BS Software Engineering, BS Data Science, and BS Artificial Intelligence (BS AI). The Electrical Engineering department offers BS Electrical Engineering and BS Computer Engineering.

Are these CS programs accredited? Yes, by the NCEAC.

What is Materials Science and Engineering at IST? It deals with the properties and applications of metals, polymers, and ceramics—vital for space tech.

Is there a Mechanical Engineering program? Yes, it is one of the core engineering departments at IST.

Does IST offer Biotechnology? Yes, IST has a specialized BS Biotechnology program.

Is IST under the military? It is a federally chartered public sector university, closely linked with SUPARCO.

What is the medium of instruction? Entirely English.

=== V. FEE STRUCTURE & FINANCIALS ===

What is the application processing fee? Approximately Rs. 3,000.

What is the per-semester tuition for Engineering? Roughly Rs. 150,000 to 165,000.

Is there an admission fee? Yes, a one-time fee of about Rs. 35,000 to 50,000.

Are there any hidden charges? Library and lab funds (usually Rs. 7,000 each) are charged once.

Can I pay in installments? You can apply for installments through the Treasurer's office in case of financial hardship.

Is the fee refundable? Tuition is refundable as per HEC policy (100% within the first week).

What is the fee for International students? Usually charged in USD (approx. $5,000+ per semester).

How do I pay the fee? Via a system-generated bank challan at any HBL branch.

What is the Endowment Fund? A small one-time contribution to the university's developmental fund.

Do I have to pay for the summer semester? Yes, summer courses are charged per credit hour.

=== VI. SCHOLARSHIPS & FINANCIAL AID ===

Does IST offer Need-Based scholarships? Yes, they participate in the HEC Need-Based Scholarship program.

What is the Honhaar Scholarship? It's a Punjab government initiative that many students use at IST.

Does IST offer Merit Scholarships? Yes, top students in each semester get tuition fee waivers.

Can I get a scholarship based on my entry test score? Usually, scholarships are awarded after enrollment based on academic performance.

Is there a PEEF scholarship? Yes, Punjab Education Endowment Fund (PEEF) is applicable.

What is the NOC for Government employees? If you are a gov worker, you must provide an NOC to be eligible for aid or admission.

Do I need to apply separately for scholarships? Usually, yes, through the Financial Aid Office (FAO).

Is financial aid available for international students? Very limited; mostly for local students.

Can scholarships be cancelled? Yes, if your GPA falls below a certain threshold (usually 2.5 or 3.0).

Does SUPARCO sponsor students? They sometimes offer sponsorships for top graduates or specific projects.

=== VII. DOCUMENTS & EQUIVALENCE ===

Do I need an IBCC equivalence for FSc? Only if you did O/A-Levels or an American High School Diploma.

Is CNIC mandatory? Yes, or Form-B if you are under 18.

What is an Attested Document? Documents signed by a Gazetted Officer or HEC/IBCC.

Do I need my original certificates for the application? No, just scanned copies. Originals are checked at the time of joining.

What is the Domicile requirement? You must have a valid Domicile for merit seats.

What if my name is different on Matric and FSc? You will need an affidavit or a revised certificate from the board.

How many photos do I need? Usually 4–8 passport-sized photos with a blue background.

Do I need a Character Certificate? Yes, from your previous institution.

What is a Migration Certificate? A document from your previous board/university allowing you to join IST.

Do I need HEC attestation for BS? Only for graduate (MS/PhD) admissions.

=== VIII. CAMPUS LIFE & HOSTELS ===

Does IST have hostels? Yes, there are furnished hostels for both boys and girls on campus.

What is the hostel fee? Roughly Rs. 45,000 to 60,000 per semester (institute figure about 45000 per semester; prospectus may cite 50,000–60,000 excluding mess).

Is the hostel mess mandatory? Usually, yes, for security and management reasons.

What is the menu in the mess? Standard Pakistani meals (Dal, Sabzi, Chicken, Roti).

Are there laundry facilities? Yes, specialized laundry areas are available in the hostels.

Is there Wi-Fi? Yes, IST provides campus-wide high-speed internet.

Where is IST located? It is situated on the Islamabad Highway (DHA Phase-II side).

Are there sports facilities? Yes, including a gym, basketball court, and football field.

Is there a transport facility? Yes, IST provides pick-and-drop for day scholars in Rawalpindi and Islamabad. For availing transport or route details call 03000544707.

What is the student life like? Highly disciplined but active with societies like the Space Society, Aero Society, etc.

=== IX. THE MERIT LIST & SELECTION ===

When are merit lists displayed? Typically in August.

How many merit lists are there? Usually 3 to 5, depending on seat availability.

What is the Closing Merit? The lowest score that got admission in the previous year.

Can I change my program if I'm in a lower merit list? Yes, if seats are available in your preferred program.

What is a Waiting List? Students who may be called if selected students don't deposit their fee.

Is there a self-finance category? IST generally operates on Open Merit; check the latest prospectus for Regular vs Payment seats.

How do I confirm my seat? By paying the fee challan within the deadline (usually 3–5 days).

Can I freeze my semester? Yes, after the first semester, you can freeze for up to a year.

What is Migration to IST? You can apply to transfer from another PEC-accredited uni after your first year.

Is there an interview for BS? Generally no for engineering; selection is based on numbers.

=== X. POST-ADMISSION & CAREER ===

When do classes start? Usually in September.

Is there an Orientation Day? Yes, a week before classes to introduce you to the campus.

What is the grading system? IST follows the GPA/CGPA system (out of 4.0).

Where do IST graduates work? SUPARCO, NESCOM, PAEC, Aviation industries, and tech companies.

Does IST help with jobs? Yes, there is a Career Development Center (CDC) and annual Job Fairs.

Can I go abroad for MS after IST? Yes, IST degrees are internationally recognized (Washington Accord).

Does IST have international collaborations? Yes, with universities in China (Beihang) and Europe.

Can I join the military after Aerospace? Yes, through the Short Service Commission (SSC) in the PAF or Navy.

Is the library 24/7? No, but it has extended hours during exams.

What if I have more questions? You can call the IST Admissions Office at +92-51-9075406 or 051-9075100.
