IST FEE STRUCTURE – Institute of Space Technology (ist.edu.pk)
All amounts in Pakistani Rupees (PKR). Say amounts in lakh and thousand (e.g. 147000 = one lakh forty seven thousand). Never use "million".
IST has 9 academic departments offering BS, MS, and PhD programs. For full list of departments and programs see IST_DEPARTMENTS_AND_PROGRAMS_SUMMARY or 05_DEPARTMENTS.

ONE-TIME CHARGES (all BS programs):
Admission fee: 35 thousand rupees. Endowment fund: 7 thousand. Library development fund: 7 thousand. Total one-time: 49 thousand rupees.

BS PROGRAMS – FEE PER SEMESTER (two semesters per year):

Department of Aeronautics and Astronautics (Aerospace Engineering): About 1 lakh 48 thousand rupees per semester (two semesters per year).

Department of Electrical Engineering: Same as Aerospace. About 1 lakh 48 thousand per semester.

Department of Avionics Engineering: Same as Aerospace. About 1 lakh 48 thousand per semester.

Department of Mechanical Engineering: Same as Aerospace. About 1 lakh 48 thousand per semester.

Department of Materials Science and Engineering: About 1 lakh 42 thousand rupees per semester.

Department of Electrical Engineering also offers BS Computer Engineering: fee is about 1 lakh 48 thousand rupees per semester (same as other engineering programs under Electrical Engineering department).

Department of Computing (BS Computer Science, BS Software Engineering, BS Artificial Intelligence, BS Data Science): About 1 lakh 26 thousand rupees per semester.

Other BS programs (Space Science, Mathematics, Physics, Biotechnology, Humanities): Generally in similar range to other BS programs; exact amount as per institute is 1 lakh 2 thousand . Admissions: 051 9075100.

BS Physics fee: 1 lakh 2 thousand rupees per semester. BS Space Science, BS Mathematics, BS Biotechnology: same, 1 lakh 2 thousand per semester.

MS PROGRAMS (graduate): Approximately 1 lakh 20 thousand rupees per year. MS offered in Aerospace, Electrical, Materials, Mechanical, Computer Science, Mathematics, Physics, Astronomy and related disciplines.

PhD PROGRAMS: Approximately 1 lakh 30 thousand rupees per year. PhD offered in Aerospace, Electrical, Materials, Mathematics, Physics, Astronomy and related disciplines.
