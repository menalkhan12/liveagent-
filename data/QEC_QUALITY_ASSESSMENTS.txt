When callers ask about "quality assessments", "QEC workshops", "quality assurance activities", or "trainings from 2011 to 2015":

IST Quality Enhancement Cell (QEC) conducts workshops, trainings, and self-assessment activities. Key activities 2011-2015:

2011: Workshop on Internal Departmental Quality Measurement (Feb); Seminar on Quality and Competitiveness (May); Training on KPIs and Data Analysis (May); Workshop on Self Assessment Report and SAR (June, Aug); Quality in Education Institutions (June); ISO 9001 awareness (Dec); Quality Tools & Techniques (Oct); Bloom's Taxonomy (Nov); Assessment of SAR (Nov); IST Feedback System (Dec).

2012: Training on Preparation of SAR (Feb, June, Aug); Assessment of SAR (Aug); Workshop on faculty in Self Assessment (Nov); World Quality Day (Dec); IST Feedback System and Bloom's Taxonomy (Dec-Jan 2013).

2013: Internal Quality Audit (Feb); Preparation of SAR (Mar, Jul, Aug); Outcome Based Education (Mar); Training for PT on SAR Criteria (May); Assessment of SAR (Aug); Effective Teaching & Assessment workshop (Jul-Aug); Importance of IST Feedback System (Dec).

2014: Preparation of SAR (Oct); Quality Management System (Nov); Internal Quality Audit (Nov); Follow-up on MS and PhD program SARs (Dec).

2015: QEC, OBE, Feedback and Bloom's Taxonomy session (Feb); Assessment of SAR (Apr); QEC and Feedback System (Oct); Preparation of Self Assessment Report (Dec).

IST follows HEC quality assurance guidelines. Engineering programs maintain PEC accreditation. Computing programs maintain NCEAC accreditation.
