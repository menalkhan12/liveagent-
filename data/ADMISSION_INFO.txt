IST offers undergraduate (BS) and graduate (MS, PhD) admissions. BS admissions are once a year (February to June for Fall intake). Merit and aggregate criteria apply; entry test is required for engineering BS programs.

Admissions office: Phone 051 9075100, or 051 9075492, 9075606, 9075472, 9075406. Email: admissions@ist.edu.pk. Online application portal: ugadmission.ist.edu.pk. Main website: ist.edu.pk/admission.

Admission and fee pages: ist.edu.pk/admission-fee-structure-bs-programs (BS fee), ist.edu.pk/admission-fee-structure-ms-local-programs (MS and PhD fee). FAQ: ist.edu.pk/admission-faq. Undergraduate info: ist.edu.pk/admission-undergraduate-info.
