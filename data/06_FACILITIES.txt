As you get involved, you'll not only have a lot of fun, you'll also develop your leadership abilities, raise your consciousness, and acquire skills you'll use after graduation. Together students, faculty and staff of IST create a welcoming and enterprising community to achieve all this.List of student societies is always evolving with the changing interests of students. The charges of the attendant will be born by students themselves.Health  SafetyIST Safety Department is responsible for developing and maintaining a safe and secure environment for the employees and students so that they can pursue their academic, professional and personal ambitions. Committed to serving with professional excellence, we strive to be approachable, reliable and truly service-oriented in the performance of our duties. The department maintains strong, professional relationships with all stake holders to ensure continuous collaboration on emergency planning efforts and familiarity with our campus in the event of an emergency.The following facilities are available at IST for Safety of individuals and premises during emergency.Fire Safety FacilitiesAll buildings of IST have dedicated Emergency Exits and are also equipped with fire extinguishers, Water Hydrants. In addition, some areas e.g. data center are equipped with latest firefighting system (FM-200), smoke and heat detectors.IST also has an in-house, highly trained firefighting team that is equipped with latest Fire Tenders and other modern firefighting equipment.Health FacilitiesHealth care is an important and necessary part of any organization. The true mark of an organization is how it looks after its employees and others.Institute of Space Technology provides medical cover to its employees and students. Going to IST is about making the most of your life as a student. As you get involved, you'll not only have a lot of fun, you'll also develop your leadership abilities, raise your consciousness, and acquire skills you'll use after graduation. Together students, faculty and staff of IST create a welcoming and enterprising community to achieve all this.
List of student societies is always evolving with the changing interests of students. Are you interested in academics, athletics, socializing, social work, performing arts, gaming or movies? There's a good chance that a student society exists for what you want to do.
Wellness and Counseling Center, IST is dedicated to help students, faculty and staff members maximize their sense of overall well-being and facilitate them towards making healthy contributions to their academic, personal, social and career development.
The Centre provides free and confidential on- campus psychological counseling services, both in individual or group settings, where the students address their concerns with a highly trained mental health clinician in a safe and non-judgmental environment.
The Centre further aims to highlight social stigma and spread awareness regarding various topics of mental health through workshops, advocacy and outreach programs.For your convenience, there are many ways to book a counseling appointment for your professional and personal concerns with either a male or a female therapist,
You can email our psychologists directly at annum.tanweer@ist.edu.pk or taj.iqbal@ist.edu.pk
You can call directly at 051-9075859 between 9 a.m to 12 p.m - Monday to Friday.
You can visit Department of Humanities and Sciences, IST or Wellness and Counseling Center at Second Floor, Rashid Block, IST.
Hostel Registration Form
Boarding facilities will be limited to students living outside Rawalpindi / Islamabad area. IST is managing 03 x hostels for boys and 1 x hostel for girls. Students are expected to bring decent colour linen and their amenities with them at the time of joining. Boarding facility will only be given depending on availability of rooms.
The cafeteria services in IST are provioded by Cafeteria Contractor on cash basis. A spcious and well managed cafeteria is available on campus. However, cafeteria services in outside hostels are provided by Cafeteria Contractor(s) and Owner of Girls Hostel for girls, on fixed monthly basis.
A well-organized Tuck Shop is available on campus which caters students needs and serves all kind of beverages and snacks etc.
Dining timings at IST and outside hostels are as under:-
Breakfast - 0700 hrs to 0800 hrs
Lunch - 1130 hrs to 1500 hrs
Dinner - 2000 hrs to 2230 hrs
Breakfast - 0730 hrs to 1000 hrs
Lunch - 1330 hrs to 1430 hrs
Dinner - 2030 hrs to 2230 hrs
A committee headed by a senior Administration member, Representatives of faculty, administration and students is constituted to check / cater the standard / quality of Cafeteria Services on campus and outside hostels.
Laundry facility is available at IST campus which caters the washing and ironing requirements of all boarding students. Fixed charges of laundry shop are paid by students on semester basis.
One attendant in each wing of the hostel would be available for cleaning of rooms and other minor chores. The charges of the attendant will be born by students themselves.
IST Safety Department is responsible for developing and maintaining a safe and secure environment for the employees and students so that they can pursue their academic, professional and personal ambitions. Committed to serving with professional excellence, we strive to be approachable, reliable and truly service-oriented in the performance of our duties. The department maintains strong, professional relationships with all stake holders to ensure continuous collaboration on emergency planning efforts and familiarity with our campus in the event of an emergency.
The following facilities are available at IST for Safety of individuals and premises during emergency.
All buildings of IST have dedicated Emergency Exits and are also equipped with fire extinguishers, Water Hydrants. In addition, some areas e.g. data center are equipped with latest firefighting system (FM-200), smoke and heat detectors.IST also has an in-house, highly trained firefighting team that is equipped with latest Fire Tenders and other modern firefighting equipment.
Health care is an important and necessary part of any organization. The true mark of an organization is how it looks after its employees and others.
Institute of Space Technology provides medical cover to its employees and students. Medical cover consists of Professional man-power, MI Room, First Aid Facility, Ambulance for 24 hours and First Aid boxes at different locations. The employees of Institute and their families are entitled to free medical treatment from panel hospitals.
IST is providing necessary Information, Instructions and training regarding Health, Safety and Environment to all at IST Campus.
