IST – DEPARTMENTS AND PROGRAMS SUMMARY (per ist.edu.pk)
================================================================================
Use this for questions like: How many departments? What programs does IST offer? List all departments / courses.

TOTAL ACADEMIC DEPARTMENTS: 9

1. Department of Aeronautics and Astronautics
2. Department of Avionics Engineering
3. Department of Electrical Engineering
4. Department of Materials Science and Engineering
5. Department of Mechanical Engineering
6. Department of Space Science
7. Department of Computing (Computer Science department)
8. Department of Applied Mathematics and Statistics
9. Department of Humanities and Sciences

In addition, IST has National Centers (e.g. National Center for Failure Analysis NCFA, National Centre for Remote Sensing and Geo Informatics). These are centers, not counted in the 9 academic departments.

UNDERGRADUATE (BS) PROGRAMS – 4-year, 8 semesters (by department):
- Aerospace Engineering (Aeronautics and Astronautics)
- Avionics Engineering (Avionics Engineering)
- Electrical Engineering (Electrical Engineering)
- Computer Engineering (Electrical Engineering)
- Mechanical Engineering (Mechanical Engineering)
- Materials Science and Engineering (Materials Science and Engineering)
- Biotechnology (Materials Science and Engineering)
- Computer Science (Computing / Computer Science department)
- Software Engineering (Computing / Computer Science department)
- Data Science (Computing / Computer Science department)
- Artificial Intelligence / BS AI (Computing / Computer Science department)
- Space Science (Space Science)
- Physics (Space Science)
- Mathematics (Applied Mathematics and Statistics)

All BS programs are HEC approved. Engineering programs are accredited by Pakistan Engineering Council (PEC).

GRADUATE PROGRAMS:
- MS (Master of Science) in various disciplines: Aerospace, Electrical, Materials, Mechanical, Computer Science, Mathematics, Physics, Astronomy and Astrophysics, and related areas.
- PhD in Aerospace, Electrical, Materials, Mathematics, Physics, Astronomy and related disciplines.

COURSES: Each department has detailed course outlines on the website (ist.edu.pk/academics). Fee structure is in FEE_STRUCTURE.txt; admissions: 051 9075100, ist.edu.pk/admission.

FACILITIES: Campus facilities include hostels (3 for boys, 1 for girls), cafeteria, tuck shop, Wellness and Counseling Center, laundry, health and safety (MI Room, ambulance, fire safety). See ist.edu.pk/campus-life/facilities-at-ist.

FACULTY: Each department has its own faculty; details are on the department pages under Academics on ist.edu.pk.

NEWS AND EVENTS: Latest news, events, and announcements are at ist.edu.pk/news-events. Research overview and labs: ist.edu.pk/research. Answer from the knowledge base; do not tell the user to check the website as the main response.
