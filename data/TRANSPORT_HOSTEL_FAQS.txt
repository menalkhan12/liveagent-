TRANSPORT:
IST offers transport on different routes. IST has its own buses. For availing transport or for route and timing details, call 03000544707.

HOSTEL:
Does IST have hostel? Yes. IST has hostels available for both girls and boys. Hostel fee per semester is 45000 rupees (45 thousand rupees). Laundry facilities are available in the hostels (laundry facilities are available in the dorms).

FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE / SCHOLARSHIPS:
Yes, scholarships are available at IST. Some are merit-based and some are need-based (financial need based). When a student asks about financial assistance or scholarships, tell them yes scholarships are available; some are merit-based and some are financial need-based.

MULTIPLE PROGRAMS / PREFERENCES:
Can I apply for more than one program? Yes, you can. You can choose preference; selection will be on merit basis.

CHANGE MAJOR AFTER ADMISSION:
Can I change my major after I'm admitted? It is possible in the first two semesters only, if you meet the required standard.

EDIT APPLICATION AFTER SUBMITTING:
Can I edit my application after submitting? Usually no, but you can upload updates to your portal before the deadline.

TEST-OPTIONAL / ENTRY TEST:
Are you test-optional? For engineering programs the entry test is mandatory (test is required). For non-engineering programs the entry test is not required.

HIGH SCHOOL CLASSES / PRE-MEDICAL / PRE-ENGINEERING:
What high school classes are required? For some degrees pre-medical (FSc pre-medical) students can apply: for example Computer Science, AI, Data Science, and Biotechnology. For most programs ICS (Intermediate in Computer Science) and pre-engineering (FSc pre-engineering) can apply to all fields.

INTERVIEW:
Do you require an interview? No, an interview is not required for admission.
